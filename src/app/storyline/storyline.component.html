Story

Crypto Collapse: The Last Transaction

Year: 2248 – Humanity is on the brink of collapse.


After years of relentless "optimization" imposed by the Trusk Governance, a global megacorporation-turned-government, the world order crumbled. Their strict guidelines, known as the "Efficiency Rules", introduced practical yet ruthless measures such as:

  • Rule #1: The Slower You Are, The Faster You're Fired.
  • Rule #2: Assets Must Flow – Hoarding Is Inefficient.
  • Rule #3: Emotions Are Costs. Cut Them.

However, it was the infamous slogan "You're Fired!" that delivered the final blow. What once was a humorous phrase became a brutal reality: entire populations were "terminated" – from jobs, cities, and ultimately, life itself.

The XXIV World War, officially referred to as "The Great Efficiency War", led to the complete collapse of financial systems. Cash? Gone. Bank accounts? Inaccessible.


What remained? Cryptocurrencies.


But even crypto has its limits now: The only way to store wealth is through hardware wallets that can hold a maximum of 100 coins. Anything beyond that is deemed inefficient and automatically erased by the system.

The Three Factions

Three powerful factions survived the war and now battle for control over the last digital assets:

  • Bot Network
    "If You're Not Automated, You're Outdated."
    An autonomous AI network that views any human decision as inefficient. Their ultimate goal? A world governed entirely by algorithms.
  • Donald Musk Holding
    "Innovation or Termination."
    A ruthless capitalist empire that exploits every opportunity for monetization. Their motto: Everything is a business – including human lives.
  • Bureaucracy Empire
    "Efficiency Through Endless Forms."
    In this faction, every action requires permits and forms. Every transaction, every movement – meticulously documented. Fail to file your paperwork on time? You're erased.

Your Mission

In this post-apocalyptic world, you take on the role of a "Ledger Runner" – a mercenary operating in the shadows, scraping together the last remaining coins. Your task is to navigate through the shattered remnants of civilization and decide:

  • Will you support one of the factions?
  • Or will you become the last independent trader in a world where every cent counts?
