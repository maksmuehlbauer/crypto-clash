Rules

Accumulate as many USD Coins as possible within 30 days by buying cryptocurrencies at low prices and selling them at higher prices for profit. Game Duration:
  • The game lasts a total of 30 days.
  • Each click on "Exchange" counts as one day passing.
Trading:
  • Buy cryptocurrencies at low prices and sell them at higher prices to make a profit.
  • Unsold cryptocurrencies at the end of the game will not be added to your final score.
Highscores:

Thetop 10 players with the highest USD Coin balances at the end of the game will be displayed on the leaderboard.


Plan your moves carefully. Every click matters, and timing your trades wisely is key to maximizing your profits!
